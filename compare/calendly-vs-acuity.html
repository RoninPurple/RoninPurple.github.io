Calendly vs Acuity

Quick answer

Calendly is best if you want a simple scheduling flow and integrations. Acuity is best if you need intake forms, packages, and more scheduling rules.

  • Simplicity: Calendly usually wins.
  • Forms + packages: Acuity usually wins.
  • Best for service businesses: Acuity often fits better when you need client intake + paid packages.
