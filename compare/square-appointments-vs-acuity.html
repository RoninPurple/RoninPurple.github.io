Square Appointments vs Acuity

Quick answer

Pick Square Appointments if you want booking + payments tightly integrated (especially if you already use Square).

Pick Acuity if you need more scheduling rules, intake forms, packages, or advanced setup.

Simple breakdown

  • Payments: Square usually wins for Square users. Acuity works well with Stripe-style setups.
  • Flexibility: Acuity usually wins (forms, packages, rules).
  • Speed to set up: Square is often faster for small shops.
