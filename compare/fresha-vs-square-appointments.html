Fresha vs Square Appointments

Quick answer

Fresha is great for salons/spas that want a strong client booking experience and marketplace discovery. Square Appointments is great if you want booking + payments tightly integrated (especially if you use Square).

  • Beauty/wellness fit: Fresha often wins.
  • Payments integration: Square often wins for Square users.
  • Speed to set up: Square is often simpler for small shops.
