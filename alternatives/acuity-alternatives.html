Acuity Scheduling Alternatives

If Acuity isn’t the perfect fit, here are solid alternatives:

  1. Square Appointments — great if you want booking + payments together.
  2. Calendly — great for simple scheduling and integrations.
  3. Fresha — popular in salons/spas; strong booking UX.
  4. Setmore — budget-friendly for small teams.
